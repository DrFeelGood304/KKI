Parvej Husen Talukder

| Founder |

Biography: Parvej Husen Talukder is a well-known young Bangladeshi poet, children's writer, freelance journalist and literary editor. He is called the king rhymester of haor region since mid-2021. He is the founder of Kavya Kishor (Also known as KKI, KKE, KKB). Parvej was born on 23 August 2005 in Jatichar village at Derai Upazila in Sunamganj of Bangladesh. He is the first child of his Father Md. Abul Kashem Talukdar and Mother Sultana Parvin. Parvej's Father is a general practitioner (Village doctor) and his mother is a housewife. Parvej is eldest of his 4 brothers. In 2012, he started to going School and in 2016, he passed Passed the Primary Education Completion Examination from Gochia Govt. Primary School. In 2019, he achieved Junior School Certificate and In 2022 he achieved Secondary School Certificate from Gochia Shamsuddin Sikandar High School. At present, he is a student of Science of Sylhet Science and Technology College, Sylhet, Bangladesh. As a Children's literary writer, Parvej regularly writes rhymes and poems in various national dailies of his country. He is also listed with 476 notable Rhymester of Bangladesh in Recently published Chorabarshiki, the Notable and largest collection of Bengali rhymes of all times. In 2021, he achieved "The King Rhymester of Haor Region" Title by many news reports of Derai as a best poet (Rhymester) of Derai Upazila (Haor Region), same year, he achieved "Tarunner Ohongkar: Parvej Husen Talukder" Title from Alor Michil Magazine. And in 2022, he achieved Jagrata Literary Honor-2022 in Children's literature Category. He also worked as Editor of Muktokathan, Literary editor of Bd-Chitro, Attrai Barta etc. In this very young age, Parvej Husen Talukder is the author of many books. Chorar Jhalak his first and remarkable rhymes book. More is Mojar Pora Chondo Chora, Smirthir Alponay Kavya, Chawa Na Chawa, And Soft Articles etc.
